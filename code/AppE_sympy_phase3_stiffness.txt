STEP 5: Torsion Scalar at O(epsilon^2)

T^2 · T^3 = (m·omega·r0·sin(theta)) · (-m·omega·r0·cos(theta))
          = -m^2·omega^2·r0^2·sin(theta)·cos(theta)

Note: The T^2·T^3 cross term with sin(theta)·cos(theta) integrates to zero
over the sphere. The document states the final form includes only sin^2(theta).

Full torsion scalar (Grok formula):
